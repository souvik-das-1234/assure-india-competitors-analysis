Actionable Next Steps

Phase 1: Foundation (0-3 Months)

  • Launch Google Ads: Immediately start PPC campaigns on brand and top 5 product keywords.
  • Build Backlinks: Secure 30+ high-quality backlinks to begin building domain authority.
  • Redesign Website: Overhaul key landing pages for a superior user experience.

Phase 2: Growth (3-6 Months)

  • Scale PPC: Expand campaigns to cover 20+ high-intent, non-brand keywords.

  • Generate Reviews: Implement a system to actively collect and display customer reviews.

Phase 3: Leadership (6+ Months)

  • Content Authority: Build a comprehensive blog/resource hub to become a thought leader.
  • Strategic Partnerships: Explore collaborations with complementary brands and retailers.
  • Remarketing: Launch campaigns to re-engage past website visitors and build loyalty.
